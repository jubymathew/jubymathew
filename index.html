About me

H Dr. JUBY MATHEW is a dynamic and resourceful teaching professional. He received his Ph.D. and Postdoc in Computer Science from Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam, Kerala. He pursued his MCA from Periyar University, Salem and M.Tech from MS University, Thirunelveli. He also holds a diploma in Cyber Law from Delhi Law University. So far, he has published his works in over fifteen international journals and presented papers in more than twenty national and international conferences. With over fifteen years of diversified teaching and corporate experience, He is actively involved in all areas of education including curriculum development, student mentoring, student career preparation and community work. Presently, he is working as an Associate Professor in MCA Department of Amal Jyothi College of Engineering Kanjirappally, Kerala. He has also authored several works covering technology and its relevance in societal expanse. Engaging PhD students of APJ Abdul Kalam University, Trivandrum as a Research Guide, he also puts in commendable service as a member of Board of Studies in the same.
